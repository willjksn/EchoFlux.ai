## Pinterest-Specific Features

### 1. AI Content Generation for Pinterest

**Pinterest requires SEO-optimized content.** AI can automatically generate:

#### Pin Titles
- **Format:** 30-60 characters (optimal for Pinterest)
- **SEO-focused:** Include keywords naturally
- **Action-oriented:** "10 Ways to...", "The Ultimate Guide to..."
- **Value-driven:** Solve user problems

**Example Output:**
```
Input: Image of a chocolate cake
Output: 
1. "Decadent Chocolate Cake Recipe | Easy Baking Guide"
2. "5-Ingredient Chocolate Cake That Melts in Your Mouth"
3. "The Perfect Chocolate Cake for Special Occasions"
```

#### Pin Descriptions
- **Length:** 150-300 words (Pinterest SEO sweet spot)
- **Structure:** 
  - First sentence: Keyword-rich hook
  - Body: Value proposition, keywords, call-to-action
  - End: Relevant keywords/hashtags
- **SEO-optimized:** Natural keyword integration

**Example:**
```
"This decadent chocolate cake recipe is perfect for any occasion. 
Made with simple ingredients you probably already have in your pantry. 
Our step-by-step guide ensures a moist, rich cake every time.

Key ingredients: dark chocolate, butter, eggs, flour, sugar.

Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or whenever you need a sweet treat.

#ChocolateCake #BakingRecipe #DessertIdeas #EasyBaking"
```

#### Keyword Research & SEO
- **Trending keywords** in user's niche
- **SEO-rich phrases** for descriptions
- **Board optimization** suggestions
- **Competition scoring** (lightweight)

#### Board Name Suggestions
- SEO-friendly board names
- Category optimization
- Keyword-rich descriptions

#### Visual Optimization
- **Aspect Ratio:** 2:3 (1000x1500px optimal)
- **High contrast** visuals
- **Minimal text** overlay (Pinterest prefers text in description)
- **Vertical format** (mobile-first)
