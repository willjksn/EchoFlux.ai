## What the App Is

**EchoFlux.ai** is an AI-powered content studio built for modern creators. It provides an all-in-one workspace for content strategy, creation, scheduling, and conversion. The platform helps creators go from idea → strategy → creation → scheduling → conversion without switching tools.

### Core Value Proposition
- **All-in-one workflow:** Strategy, creation, scheduling, and conversion in one place
- **AI-powered speed:** Generate content in your voice, aligned with your goals
- **Brand consistency:** Every AI output respects your niche, tone, and objectives
- **Conversion focus:** Tools to turn engagement into owned audiences

## Core Features Breakdown

### A) Dashboard — Central Command

**What It Is:**  
The main hub that shows what matters this week and provides quick access to key workflows.

**How It Works:**
- Displays upcoming content and priorities
- Shows quick access to planning and creation tools
- Serves as the central starting point for weekly work

**Key Sections:**
- Weekly overview and priorities
- Quick actions for common tasks
- Focus areas and recommendations

**Use Case:**  
Users start here to see what needs attention, then navigate to specific tools (Plan My Week, Write Captions, etc.).

---

### B) Plan My Week — AI Strategy Engine

**What It Is:**  
The strategy generator that creates multi-week content roadmaps tailored to your niche, goals, and tone.

**How It Works:**
1. User inputs:
   - Goals (e.g., "increase engagement," "launch new product")
   - Tone and voice preferences
   - Platform focus (Instagram, TikTok, X, etc.)
   - Desired cadence

2. AI generates:
   - Multi-week content roadmap
   - Topic themes and post ideas
   - Suggested content types (educational, entertaining, promotional)
   - Calendar-ready structure

3. Outputs feed into:
   - Drafts workflow
   - My Schedule for scheduling
   - Write Captions for detailed caption generation

**Use Case:**  
Creators use this to generate a complete content plan for 2-4 weeks, then execute by moving ideas into Drafts and scheduling them.

---

### C) Write Captions — AI Content Creation Hub

**What It Is:**  
The central content creation workspace where users generate, refine, and optimize captions for their posts.

**How It Works:**

#### 1. **Caption Generation**
- User provides a brief prompt or idea
- AI generates captions in the user's voice and tone
- Outputs respect brand guidelines and goals

#### 2. **Caption Optimizer** (New AI Intelligence Feature)
- Analyzes existing captions for improvement opportunities
- Optimizes for:
  - Hook strength (first line impact)
  - CTA clarity
  - Hashtag strategy
  - Platform-specific length
  - Engagement triggers
- Provides before/after comparison with explanations

#### 3. **Content Repurposing** (New AI Intelligence Feature)
- Takes one caption and adapts it for multiple platforms
- Generates platform-optimized versions for:
  - Instagram (captions, Reels, Stories)
  - TikTok
  - X (Twitter)
  - LinkedIn
  - YouTube
- Includes platform-specific hashtag strategies and format recommendations

#### 4. **Performance Predictor** (New AI Intelligence Feature)
- Predicts content performance BEFORE posting (High/Medium/Low)
- Analyzes:
  - Caption quality
  - Platform optimization
  - Timing (if scheduled)
  - Content type alignment
  - Trend alignment
  - Historical patterns
- Suggests improvements to boost prediction
- Provides optimized version with expected performance boost

**Use Case:**  
Creators write captions here, use AI tools to refine them, repurpose for multiple platforms, and predict performance before finalizing.

**Key Workflow:**
1. Generate caption from prompt
2. Optimize for better hooks/CTAs
3. Repurpose for multiple platforms
4. Predict performance
5. Move to Drafts for approval
6. Schedule via My Schedule

---

### D) My Vault — Media Library

**What It Is:**  
Centralized storage for all media assets (images and videos).

**How It Works:**
- Upload images and videos directly to the vault
- Organize assets with folders and tags
- Attach media from vault to content in Drafts or Write Captions
- Track storage usage against plan limits

**Features:**
- Drag-and-drop upload
- Folder organization
- Search and filter
- Storage quota management

**Use Case:**  
Users upload and organize all their media assets here, then attach them to posts when creating content in Write Captions or Drafts.

---

### E) Drafts — Approval/Workflow Board

**What It Is:**  
A workflow stage where content moves from idea to post-ready.

**How It Works:**
- Kanban-style board with stages:
  - **Draft** — Initial content creation
  - **Review** — Content being reviewed/edited
  - **Approved** — Ready to schedule/post

- Actions available:
  - Edit captions and content
  - Attach media from My Vault
  - Move between stages
  - Schedule directly to My Schedule
  - Delete unwanted drafts

**Use Case:**  
Content moves through Drafts → Review → Approved, then gets scheduled via My Schedule. This ensures quality control before publishing.

**Plan Access:**  
Available for Elite and Agency plans.

---

### F) My Schedule — Visual Content Calendar

**What It Is:**  
A visual calendar interface for planning and scheduling content across days and weeks.

**How It Works:**
- Visual calendar view (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Drag-and-drop content from Drafts onto calendar dates
- Click any scheduled post to:
  - Edit caption or content
  - Adjust timing
  - Swap attached media
  - View preview

**Features:**
- Multi-week view for planning ahead
- Platform indicators for each post
- Time slots for optimal posting
- Gap detection (missing days)

**Use Case:**  
Users drag approved content from Drafts onto the calendar, adjust timing, and maintain a consistent posting cadence across weeks.

**Plan Access:**  
Available for Pro, Elite, and Agency plans (Free plan cannot access).

---

### G) What's Working — Analytics View

**What It Is:**  
Performance analytics and insights to understand what content resonates with audiences.

**How It Works:**
- Displays engagement metrics and performance data
- Shows trends in content performance
- Highlights top-performing content types
- Provides insights to inform future content strategy

**Key Metrics:**
- Engagement rates
- Performance trends
- Top content types
- Audience insights

**Use Case:**  
Creators review What's Working to understand what's resonating, then use those insights to guide their next Plan My Week generation.

**Note:**  
In offline mode, this may show limited data (relies on live social connections for real-time analytics).

---

### H) Find Trends — Opportunities

**What It Is:**  
Trend discovery and opportunity finder to identify timely content ideas.

**How It Works:**
- Surfaces trending topics, hashtags, and content angles
- Identifies opportunities aligned to user's niche
- Provides actionable trend insights
- Updates regularly to stay current

**Features:**
- Trending hashtags in your niche
- Viral content ideas
- Popular topics
- Collaboration opportunities

**Use Case:**  
Creators use Find Trends to discover timely angles and topics, then incorporate them into their Plan My Week strategy.

**Plan Access:**  
Available for Pro, Elite, and Agency plans (not available for Free plan).

### I) Bio Link Page — Conversion Hub

**What It Is:**  
A link-in-bio builder that creates branded landing pages with email capture functionality.

**How It Works:**

#### 1. **Page Builder**
- Customize colors, fonts, and layout
- Add profile photo and bio
- Create branded, mobile-optimized pages

#### 2. **Link Management**
- Add multiple links (products, social profiles, offers)
- Organize links into sections
- Set custom link labels and icons

#### 3. **Email Capture**
- Built-in email capture form
- Automatic subscriber list management
- Export subscriber emails (Admin can access)

#### 4. **Public URL**
- Each user gets a unique public URL
- Format: `yoursite.com/username` or `/u/username`
- Share this link in social media bios

**Plan Limits:**
- **Free:** 1 link
- **Pro:** 5 links
- **Elite:** Unlimited links

**Use Case:**  
Creators build a branded bio page, add links to offers/products, capture emails from visitors, and share the URL in their social bios to convert engagement into owned audiences.

A premium, all-in-one workspace specifically designed for monetized creators on platforms like OnlyFans, Fansly, Fanvue, and similar premium content platforms.

#### 4. **Fan Management**
- Track VIPs and high-intent followers

- Prioritize high-value fans in engagement workflows

#### 5. **Export Hub**
- Generate export packages for ready-to-upload content sets
- Create Funnel Teaser Packs for cross-platform promo content

**Use Case:**  
Elite creators use the Premium Content Studio to generate revenue-focused content, manage fan engagement, prioritize VIPs, and export ready-to-upload packages for premium platforms.

**Plan Access:**  
Available for Elite and OnlyFans Studio plans (included with Elite).

#### 4. **Preferences**

- Notification settings
- Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
- Storage quota management

#### 5. **Referral Program**
- View referral code
- Track referrals and earned rewards
- Claim milestone rewards (extra generations, free months, storage boosts)

**Use Case:**  
Users configure their AI personality here so all generated content (captions, strategies, etc.) matches their brand voice and goals.

## Admin Features

### Admin Dashboard

**What It Is:**  
Comprehensive admin control center for managing the entire platform.

**Key Tabs:**

#### 1. **Overview**
- Total users count
- Revenue metrics (MRR simulation)
- New users (30 days)
- Plan distribution
- Top users by AI generations
- Recent activity feed
- Model usage analytics

#### 2. **Users**
- User list with search and filters
- Plan management (upgrade/downgrade)
- Usage statistics per user
- Storage usage tracking
- Edit user details
- Grant individual rewards

#### 3. **Waitlist**
- View pending, approved, and rejected waitlist entries
- Approve users (sends email with invite code)
- Reject or delete entries
- Email status tracking
- Automatic email templates

#### 4. **Bio Emails**
- View all email subscribers from bio pages
- Filter by creator
- Export subscriber list to CSV
- See subscription date and source

#### 5. **Announcements**
- Create and manage announcements
- Public banner announcements (shown on landing page)
- In-app banner announcements (targeted by plan)
- Reminders dropdown for logged-in users
- Schedule announcements with start/end dates
- Target specific plans (Free, Pro, Elite)

#### 6. **Promotions**
- Create discount codes (percentage, fixed amount, or free access)
- Set usage limits (total and per-user)
- Schedule promotions with date ranges
- Plan-specific promotions
- Track promotion usage
- Activate/deactivate promotions

#### 7. **Referral Rewards**
- Configure milestone rewards (1, 3, 5, 10, 20 referrals)
- Set reward types:
  - Extra AI Generations
  - Free Month(s)
  - Storage Boost (GB)
- Grant individual rewards
- Bulk grants by plan
- Promo cohort grants (reward users who upgraded during announcement window)

#### 8. **Tools**
- Mass Email Composer
- Other admin utilities

### Email Center (Admin-Only)

**What It Is:**  
Central hub for all email operations and management.

**Tabs:**

#### 1. **Inbox**
- View incoming support/contact messages
- Reply to user inquiries
- Manage support tickets

#### 2. **Sent**
- View all sent emails
- Track email delivery status
- View email history

#### 3. **Failed**
- View failed email attempts

- Retry failed sends

#### 4. **Tools**
- **Send:** Single email composer for one-off emails
- **Templates:** Manage reusable email templates

- **Bio Emails:** View and manage bio page subscribers
