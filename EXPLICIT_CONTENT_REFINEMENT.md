## AI Understanding

### What the AI Now Knows

1. **Analyze Media First**
   - Look at what's actually shown in image/video
   - Describe explicit/intimate content visible
   - Focus on visual details, not generic prompts

2. **Describe, Don't Just Promote**
   - Captions describe what's shown explicitly
   - Topics describe specific content to be created
   - Image/video ideas are detailed visual concepts

3. **Natural Monetization**
   - Subtle hints, not constant sales pitches
   - Focus on content quality, not just subscriptions
   - Let the content speak for itself

4. **No Platform Names**
   - Don't mention OnlyFans/Fanvue in captions
   - Keep captions natural and platform-agnostic
   - Focus on content, not platform promotion
