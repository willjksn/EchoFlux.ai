## Full Product Description (for Stripe product metadata or marketing copy)

### What is EchoFlux.ai?

EchoFlux.ai is an AI-powered content studio and campaign planner designed specifically for content creators, influencers, and OnlyFans creators. It replaces the need for multiple fragmented tools by providing a unified platform for content strategy, creation, planning, and organization.

### Core Value Proposition

**Stop juggling 10 different tools. One AI-powered platform for everything.**

EchoFlux.ai helps creators:
- **Plan smarter** with AI-generated multi-week content strategies
- **Create faster** with AI captions, hashtags, and content ideas
- **Stay organized** with visual calendars and media libraries
- **Grow efficiently** with link-in-bio builders and trend research

### Current Mode: Planning-First Studio

EchoFlux.ai currently operates as an **AI Content Studio** focused on planning and content creation. Users create, plan, and organize content within the platform, then copy content to post manually on their preferred social platforms. This approach gives creators full control while leveraging AI for strategy and content generation.

### Pricing Tiers

- **Free**: 1 strategy/month, 10 captions/month, 1 link-in-bio, 100 MB storage
- **Pro ($29/mo)**: 2 strategies/month, 500 captions/month, 5 links, live trends, 5 GB storage
- **Elite ($59/mo)**: 5 strategies/month, 1,500 captions/month, unlimited links, enhanced trends, OnlyFans Studio, 10 GB storage

### Technical Details

- **Platform**: Web-based SaaS application
- **Deployment**: Vercel-hosted React application
- **Backend**: Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Auth)
- **AI Models**: Google Gemini for content generation
- **Trend Research**: Tavily API for live web search
- **Storage**: Firebase Storage for media files
