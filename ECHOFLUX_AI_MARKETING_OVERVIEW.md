## üéØ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**EchoFlux.ai** is an **AI Content Studio & Campaign Planner** built exclusively for creators. It's a centralized planning and content generation platform that helps creators plan, create, and organize content across all major social platforms without requiring social account connections.

**Core Philosophy:** Planning-first, manual publishing. Creators generate content in EchoFlux.ai, then copy and post manually to their platforms for maximum control.

**Target Audience:** Social media creators, influencers, content creators, premium content creators (OnlyFans/Fansly/Fanvue), and personal brands looking to scale their content strategy.

## üíé CORE FEATURES & HOW THEY WORK

### 1. **AI Content Strategist (Strategy Page)**
**What it does:** Generates multi-week content roadmaps with campaigns, themes, and post ideas.

**How it works:**
- Creator enters: Niche (e.g., "women's fitness"), Target Audience (e.g., "women 25-35 interested in wellness"), Goal (Growth/Sales/Engagement), Duration (1-4 weeks), Tone, Platform Focus
- AI generates: Complete multi-week roadmap with weekly themes, daily post ideas, campaign concepts, and strategic recommendations
- Creator uses: Strategy as blueprint ‚Üí uses post ideas in Compose to create actual content ‚Üí schedules on Calendar

**Plan Limits:**
- Free: 1 basic strategy/month
- Pro: 2 strategies/month  
- Elite: 5 strategies/month

**Value Proposition:** "Never wonder 'what should I post today?' again. Get a complete month of content ideas in seconds."

---

### 2. **AI Content Generation (Compose Page)**
**What it does:** Generates platform-optimized captions from simple ideas.

**How it works:**
- Creator uploads media (optional) OR types content idea
- Selects platforms (Instagram, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Chooses Goal (Engagement/Sales/Growth) and Tone (Professional/Friendly/Casual/Witty/Inspirational/Sexy/Explicit)
- AI generates multiple caption options, each automatically optimized for selected platforms:
  - **Instagram:** Character limits, hashtag placement, engagement hooks
  - **TikTok:** Hook-first format, trending style, call-to-action
  - **X (Twitter):** Concise, thread-friendly, engagement-driven
  - **LinkedIn:** Professional, value-focused, network-building

**Advanced Features:**
- **Content Gap Analysis:** AI analyzes strategy to identify missing content types/topics
- **Hashtag Manager:** Save and reuse hashtag sets per platform
- **History:** Track all generated content

**Plan Limits:**
- Free: 10 AI captions/month
- Pro: 500 AI captions/month
- Elite: 1,500 AI captions/month

**Value Proposition:** "Create Instagram, TikTok, and X captions from one idea in 30 seconds. Each optimized for platform best practices."

---

### 3. **Opportunities (Trend Discovery)**
**What it does:** Scans for trending topics, hashtags, and content opportunities in creator's niche.

**How it works:**
- Creator enters niche (e.g., "fitness", "beauty", "gaming")
- AI scans and returns trending hashtags, content angles, engagement opportunities
- Creator can: Generate content from trends, save hashtags, use in Compose, create strategy from trend

**Plan Availability:** Pro, Elite plans

**Value Proposition:** "Ride trends before they peak. Get trending hashtags and content angles tailored to your niche."
