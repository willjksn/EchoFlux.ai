# Meta App Details Form - How to Fill Out for EngageSuite.ai

When Meta asks for app details during creation, here's what to enter:

---

## App Details Form Fields

### **App Name**
```
EngageSuite.ai
```
(Or whatever you want to name it)

### **App Contact Email**
```
your-email@example.com
```
(Your actual email address)

### **Use Case / What does your app do?**

**Short version:**
```
Social media management and content creation platform that helps creators and businesses manage their Instagram and Facebook accounts, schedule posts, analyze performance, and engage with their audience.
```

**Detailed version (if they want more):**
```
EngageSuite.ai is a comprehensive social media management platform designed for content creators and businesses. The app allows users to:
- Connect and manage their Instagram and Facebook accounts
- Create, schedule, and publish social media content
- Analyze performance metrics and engagement
- Manage messages and respond to comments
- Generate AI-powered captions and content suggestions
- Track audience growth and engagement trends
```

### **Business / Company Information**

**Business Name:**
```
EngageSuite.ai
```
(Or your company name if different)

**Website:**
```
https://engagesuite.ai
```
(Or your actual website URL)

**Business Category:**
- Select **"Business"** or **"Marketing"** or **"Productivity"**

### **Requirements / Permissions Needed**

Select or mention:
- ✅ **Instagram Basic Display API** - To connect user's Instagram account and access their profile/media
- ✅ **Facebook Login** (if implementing) - To connect Facebook accounts
- ✅ **Access user profile information** - To display connected accounts

**Why you need these permissions:**
```
To provide users with a unified dashboard to manage their Instagram and Facebook accounts, access their content and engagement data, and enable them to publish and schedule posts across platforms.
```

### **Overview / App Description**

**Short description:**
```
A social media management platform that helps creators and businesses streamline their Instagram and Facebook content creation, scheduling, and analytics in one place.
```

**Longer description (if they ask for more):**
```
EngageSuite.ai empowers content creators and businesses to efficiently manage their social media presence. Users can connect their Instagram and Facebook accounts to access a unified dashboard where they can:

- Create and schedule posts with AI-powered caption generation
- View real-time analytics and engagement metrics
- Manage direct messages and comments from a central inbox
- Track audience growth and performance trends
- Automate content strategies with AI-powered suggestions

The app requires Instagram Basic Display API access to enable users to connect their accounts and manage their social media presence seamlessly.
```

---

## Key Points to Emphasize

1. **Clear Purpose**: Social media management tool
2. **User Benefit**: Helps users manage multiple social accounts efficiently
3. **Privacy**: Only accesses data users explicitly authorize
4. **Compliance**: Mention that users control what they share

---

## After Submitting the Form

Once you submit this form:
1. Meta will create your app
2. You'll be taken to the app dashboard
3. Then you can proceed with adding Instagram Basic Display product
4. Continue with the rest of the setup guide

---

## Important Notes

- **Be honest and clear** about what your app does
- **Don't request permissions you don't need** - only Instagram Basic Display for now
- **For testing**: You can submit in Development Mode - you don't need full app review for testing with testers
- **Later for production**: You'll need to submit for App Review if you want to go public
