## üìã Production Guidelines

### Visual Requirements:
- Use actual EchoFlux.ai app screenshots/recordings
- Maintain brand consistency (colors, fonts, styling)
- Fast-paced cuts (1-2 seconds per shot)
- Product-focused (show real interface)
- Mobile-optimized text overlays

### Audio Requirements:
- Professional voiceover (clear, confident, energetic)
- Upbeat background music (instrumental, modern)
- Fast but clear pacing
- Appropriate tone for audience

### Text Overlays:
- Large, readable fonts (sans-serif)
- High contrast colors
- Smooth animations
- Brief duration (2-3 seconds max)

### Platform Adaptations:
- **Horizontal (16:9):** YouTube, website, presentations
- **Vertical (9:16):** Instagram Stories, TikTok, Snapchat
- **Square (1:1):** Instagram feed, Facebook
