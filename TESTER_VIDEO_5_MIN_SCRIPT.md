## Digital Creator (Mixed Platforms) — 0:50–3:40

### 0:50–1:55 — Strategy (Digital creator roadmap)
**Say:**  
“Start in Strategy when you want a clear roadmap. I’m using the niche ‘digital creator’—content systems, creator economy, and tools. Enter niche, audience, goal, and platforms—then generate your roadmap.”

**Do (fast inputs):**
- **Niche**: Digital creator (content creation, creator economy, tools)  
- **Audience**: Aspiring creators + small creators who want systems  
- **Goal**: Grow audience + drive email signups  
- **Platform focus**: Mixed (IG + TikTok + X)  
- Click **Generate Strategy**
- Scroll **2–3 seconds** to show weeks/days structure
- Highlight one day card/row briefly (don’t deep-dive)

---

### 1:55–2:55 — Compose (Generate captions from an image)
**Say:**  
“Compose is where you generate and refine captions. Upload an image or video, pick goal and tone, select platforms, and generate caption options tailored to each platform.”

**Do:**
- Click **Compose**
- Upload a sample image (creator toolkit / workflow / Notion dashboard screenshot works well)
- Select **platforms** (Instagram + TikTok + X)
- Select **goal** and **tone**
- Click **Generate Captions**
- Pick one result and make a **tiny edit** (1 short sentence)

---

### 2:55–3:40 — Calendar (Plan + edit + export)
**Say:**  
“Calendar is your planning board. Click a post to preview, edit, and save changes—then export the caption so you can post anywhere.”

**Do:**
- Click **Calendar**
- Click a planned item to open preview
- Click **Edit** → make a small change → **Save Changes**
- Click **Export** → **Copy caption** (or download text)

---
